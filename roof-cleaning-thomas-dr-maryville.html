The Soft Wash Roof Cleaning Method

TN Exterior Cleaning Services uses the industry-leading soft wash method, which is the safest and most effective approach for roof cleaning:

Professional Soft Wash Process Steps:

  • Initial Property Assessment: Thorough inspection of your roof condition, type, and specific cleaning needs
  • Surrounding Protection: Careful protection of landscaping, gutters, and surrounding areas
  • Pre-Treatment Application: Professional-grade cleaning solution applied to kill algae, moss, and mildew at the root
  • Dwell Time: Solution allowed to work (typically 10-15 minutes) to break down organic growth
  • Low-Pressure Rinse: Gentle, low-pressure water rinse (under 500 PSI) that safely removes all contaminants
  • Post-Treatment Inspection: Careful review to ensure complete cleaning and no damage
  • Gutter Flush: Flushing gutters and downspouts to remove all debris and cleaning solution
  • Final Walkthrough: Customer walkthrough to review results and answer questions
  • Maintenance Recommendations: Advice on keeping your roof clean longer and scheduling future service

Why Soft Wash is Superior to Pressure Washing

Aspect Soft Wash (Recommended) Pressure Washing
Pressure Used Under 500 PSI (gentle) 1,500-3,000+ PSI (harsh)
Shingle Safety Protects granules and shingles Can strip granules and damage shingles
Cleaning Method Chemical treatment kills growth at root Physical force washes surface only
Results Duration 2-3 years (kills spores) 6-12 months (surface cleaning only)
Warranty Impact Maintains roof warranty Often voids manufacturer warranty
Roof Lifespan Extends roof life Can shorten roof life
Recommended by Manufacturers Yes - approved method No - often prohibited

Important: Pressure Washing Warning

Many roofing manufacturers explicitly void warranties if high-pressure washing is used. The intense pressure can:

  • Strip protective granules from asphalt shingles
  • Force water under shingles causing leaks
  • Damage flashing and sealants
  • Reduce your roof's expected lifespan by years
  • Require premature roof replacement

Always choose soft wash cleaning for your Thomas Dr home to protect your investment.

Maintaining Your Clean Roof Between Professional Services

Simple maintenance steps help Thomas Dr homeowners extend the life of their professional roof cleaning and protect their investment.

Easy Maintenance Tips

Between Cleaning Maintenance:

  • Trim Overhanging Branches: Reduce shade and debris accumulation on your roof
  • Clean Gutters Regularly: Prevent water backup and moisture issues (every 6 months)
  • Remove Debris Promptly: Clear leaves, branches, and organic matter quickly
  • Monitor for Early Growth: Address small algae spots before they spread extensively
  • Improve Attic Ventilation: Proper airflow reduces moisture retention
  • Install Zinc Strips: One-time investment helps prevent algae regrowth
  • Regular Visual Inspections: Check your roof every few months from the ground
  • Document Changes: Take photos to track any growth or changes
